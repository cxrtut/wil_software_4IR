Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RentBox policies and services.

What types of equipment does RentBox offer?

We offer a variety of event equipment including tents, tables, seating, decor, catering supplies, lighting, and sound systems. Check our catalog for more details and availability.

What is the rental period for equipment?

The standard rental period is 48 hours, but we offer flexible options if you need equipment for a longer duration. Contact us for special arrangements.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply login or create an account then select the equipment you want, choose your rental dates, and proceed to checkout.

What are the rental fees and payment options?

Rental fees vary by equipment type and duration. We accept payments by credit/debit card, EFT, and other methods listed at checkout. Full payment is required to confirm a booking and an insurance amount.

How does RentBox handle delivery and pickup?

We offer delivery and pickup services across South Africa. Delivery fees are based on location, and details are available during the booking process. Customers can also opt for self-collection and return.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation within 48 hours of your rental date for a full refund.

What happens if equipment is damaged?

If equipment is damaged during the rental period, additional fees may apply. We encourage clients to inspect equipment upon delivery. Damaged or missing items are assessed after each rental period.
